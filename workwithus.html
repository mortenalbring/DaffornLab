PhD projects

We admit new PhD students nearly every year through the UoB MIBTP programme amongst others. Projects are advertised when they are available but informal enquiries are welcome too.

The polymers we use are based on styrene-maleic acid (SMA). SMA-lipid nanoparticles (SMALPs) self-assemble when the amphipathic styrene-maleic acid co-polymer is mixed with cell membranes, enabling it to encapsulate and stabilise membrane proteins along with their native membrane lipids.
